1.AUDIO STREAMER

  • First month and second month of your Quota
  • BEANS REQUIRED (350K) BEANS
  • HOURS REQUIRED (50) Hours
  • DAYS REQUIRED (15) Days
  • For the third month of your quota
  • BEANS REQUIRED (600K) BEANS
  • HOURS REQUIRED (50) Hours
  • DAYS REQUIRED (15) Days

1.1 AUDIO

  • Always launch the calculator in order for us to credit the gifts and giveaways of the guest.
  • Avoid using mute while you are a host in your room.
  • Avoid kicking our fellow streamer, but if it’s rude, you can report it and kick it to your room
  • Always check the age of the streamer sitting in your bubbles. You can talk to them to change the age and go down first, but if they don’t listen to you, you can remove them from the bubbles.
  • Always check if the picture of the streamer you are talking to is a child or a baby you can talk to him and change the picture to his real picture but if doesn’t listen to you, you can tell him to take it down first and come back when you have change.
  • Do not mention other application similar to Hago.
  • Do not endorse any product while streaming.

2.VIDEO STREAMER

  • First month and second month of your Quota
  • BEANS REQUIRED (420K) BEANS
  • DAYS REQUIRED (17) Days
  • HOURS REQUIRED (35) Hours
  • For the third month of your quota
  • BEANS REQUIRED (650K) BEANS
  • DAYS REQUIRED (17) Days
  • HOURS REQUIRED (35) Hours

2.1 VIDEO STREAMER

  • Avoid kicking our fellow streamer, but if it’s rude, you can report it and kick it to your room
  • Always check the age of the streamer sitting in your guest.
  • Avoid seeing children or babies in your streaming.
  • Avoid eating while on a live stream.
  • Wear Proper Clothes and not the rude ones.
  • Do not endorse any product while streaming.
  • Don’t show any deadly weapon while streaming.
  • Do not show sensitive parts of your body.
  • Do not dance an obscene dance.
  • Provide quality content to your viewers so that they will be satisfied with you.
